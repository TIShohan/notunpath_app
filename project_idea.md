Project Description *
An awareness based mobile application called নতুনপথ will be created to fill the gaps in Bangladesh's adolescent health education and support system. The app addresses culturally sensitive subjects by offering a secure, approachable, and friendly-well structural system, and it will be targeted mostly for now teens and parents. নতুনপথ offers structured modules on relationship-building, reproductive health, mental wellness, and adolescent changes. Users can consult healthcare professionals and psychologists through online and even make in-person appointments with privacy protections by anonymous communication channel. Through a database of commonly asked questions, the app's AI buddy provides initial assistance, filling in the gaps for users looking for quick answers on basic queries.

Objective (Main Goal) *
1. Spread Awareness and Knowledge: Give teenagers easy-to-access, factual information about gender-based understanding, mental health, reproductive health, and puberty.

2. Support Parents and Families: Give parents direction on how to talk sensitively about difficult subjects with their kids, so promoting better family communication and closer relationships.

3. Break Cultural Taboos: Reduce stigma and cultivate healthier societal attitudes by raising knowledge and promoting comfort with age-appropriate health education in a culturally relevant manner.

4. Allows Private Professional Aid: Make expert assistance available and enable users to consult with doctors and psychologists in an anonymous manner, addressing personal matters without shame.

5. AI-Buddy: Provide an AI buddy that answers common questions related to adolescent health and mental wellness, guiding users toward more detailed expert consultations as needed.
