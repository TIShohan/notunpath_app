- 20% of Bangladesh's population are adolescents (ages 10-19)
- Critical health topics are taboo in society
- Limited access to reliable health information

 The Opportunity
- Rising smartphone penetration
- Need for anonymous, accessible health education

 Slide 3: Problem Statement

 6 Critical Problems

1. Cultural Taboos - Shame around puberty, menstruation, mental health
2. Information Gap - No reliable, age-appropriate resources
3. Professional Support Deficit - Limited access to specialists
4. Parent-Teen Barrier - Difficulty discussing sensitive topics
5. Mental Health Neglect - Stigma prevents seeking help
6. Privacy Concerns - Fear of judgment
